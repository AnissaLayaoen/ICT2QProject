Banaue Rice Terraces
  • The Banaue Rice Terraces are the world's oldest rice terraces and are considered to be the 8th Natural Wonder of the World by many Filipinos.
  • It's irrigation system was very efficient, despite it being very old.
  • The terraces has hiking trails to guide tourists and provides many spectacular views.
Mayon Volcano
  • The Mayon Volcano is located in Albay, Bicol.
  • It is known for its "perfect cone" shape due to the symmetry of it's shape.
Bohol: Chocolate Hills
  • The Chocolate Hills turn brown during the hot dry season, creating a breathtaking scenery with infinite hills.
